AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

We are set to implement all the laudable programmes as enunciated in the national policy on education (1981)

  • To equip students to live effectively in the modern world of science and technology.
  • To develop and project Nigerians culture, and language as well as the Universal and cultural heritage.
  • To raise a generation of people who can think for themselves, respects the views and dignity of labour and appreciate those values specified under our broad national aims so as to enable them lives and function as good citizens
  • To foster Nigerian unity based on justice and far play.
  • To Inspire students with a desire for achievement and self improvement both at school and later on in life

    ACTS CONSIDERED TO CONSTITUTES MISCONDUCT ON THE PART OF STUDENTS OF MODEL "HIS & HERS" COLLEGE

  • IMPROPER DRESSING: Proper dressing entail plaiting of the approved hair styles by the female students and the barbing of cut hair style by the mai student The school uniform consist of pink barged gown with a light check pink tie & beret to match The boys are expected to be dressed in the same light pink check as shirt and pink short with te to match The use of hair attachment is prohibited. A week or fortnightly hairstyle will be presented to the female students and this should be strictly complied with
  • The use of cell-phones during the class activities a not condoned
  • Insubordination or Rudeness to any Members of Staff and School Prefect
  • Fighting Stealing. Lying and Loitering
  • Failure to do Home-work, Class work/Assignment. Late coming and other forms of Truancy
  • Exit from the School or the Classroom without. permission
  • Eating at the School Canteen should be done during the break period
  • Acts of environment untidiness and failure to observe cleanliness exercises in the School compound
  • Acts Capable of tarnishing the image of the School and inciting other students against the Staff and School Authority

    • PENALTIES FOR STUDENT MISCONDUCT

    The Management of the School shall implement strictly its disciplinary measure against any student found to be violating the laid down rules and regulations depending on the gravity of the offense so committed such as

  • Advice/Admonition
  • Corporal Punishment such as caning on the palm or buttocks.
  • Manual labour, such as cutting or weeding of grasses. sweeping, fetching of water etc, during the long break or after the school hours
  • Severe cases of student(s) misconducts may warrant the student(s) being suspended for a period of time and the invitation of the student's parents to sign an undertaking to be of good behavior. After the period of the suspension
  • As a last resort, a very serious case of misconduct shall attract dismissal, if so recommended by the disciplinary committee and endorsed by the school authority/proprietor
